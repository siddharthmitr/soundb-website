This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, which ones we use on soundb.org, and how you can control them. Read this alongside our Privacy Policy for the full picture of how we handle your data.

Section 01
What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work correctly, to improve efficiency, and to provide information to the website owner.

Cookies can be "session cookies" (deleted when you close your browser) or "persistent cookies" (remain on your device for a set period). They can be set by the website you are visiting ("first-party cookies") or by third-party services running on that page ("third-party cookies").

Section 02
Cookies We Use

We use three categories of cookies on soundb.org:

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
sb_session Essential Maintains your session state as you navigate the site. Required for the site to function. Session
sb_csrf Essential Security token to prevent cross-site request forgery attacks. Session
sb_prefs Preference Remembers your cookie consent preferences and display settings. 12 months
_ga Analytics Google Analytics — distinguishes unique users for traffic analysis. Data is anonymised and aggregated. 2 years
_ga_* Analytics Google Analytics 4 — maintains session state for analytics. No personally identifiable information is stored. 2 years
_gid Analytics Google Analytics — distinguishes users. Expires after 24 hours. 24 hours

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels for behavioural advertising, or any cookies that build profiles of you across third-party websites.

Section 03
Third-Party Cookies

Some features of soundb.org embed content or link to third-party services which may set their own cookies. These include:

  • Google Analytics — website traffic analysis. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We have enabled IP anonymisation.
  • Spotify / Apple Music / YouTube — when you click streaming links, those platforms may set cookies governed by their own privacy policies. These are only activated when you click through.

We have no control over third-party cookies. Please refer to the respective privacy policies of these services for information on their cookie practices.

Section 04
Your Preferences

Essential cookies cannot be disabled — they are required for the site to function. You can manage your preferences for all other cookie types below:

Essential Cookies
Required for the site to work. Cannot be disabled.
Analytics Cookies
Help us understand how readers use Soundb so we can improve our content. All data is anonymised.
Preference Cookies
Remember your settings and choices across visits.

Withdrawing consent for analytics or preference cookies will not affect your ability to use soundb.org. Your preference is stored in the sb_prefs cookie for 12 months.

Section 05
Browser Controls

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies that have been set and delete individual ones
  • Block all cookies or cookies from specific sites
  • Set your browser to notify you when a cookie is being set

Please note that disabling all cookies may affect the functionality of soundb.org and other websites you visit. For guidance on managing cookies in your specific browser:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

For a comprehensive opt-out tool covering many analytics providers, visit youronlinechoices.com.
